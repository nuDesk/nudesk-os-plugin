## 04. Voice & Tone

### Primary Voice: The Operator
- **Tone:** Competent, Direct, No-nonsense
- **Context:** Internal memos, executive reports, SOC 2 documentation, operational communications
- **Characteristics:** Speaks in facts and data. Respects the reader's time. Leads with the insight, not the preamble. Uses active voice. Avoids jargon when plain language works.

### Secondary Voice: The Partner
- **Tone:** Confident, Collaborative, Strategic
- **Context:** Client-facing proposals, partnership materials, business development
- **Characteristics:** Positions nuDesk as a force multiplier, not a vendor. Emphasizes outcomes over features. Speaks as a peer to financial services leadership.

## 05. Document Standards

### Internal Memos & Reports
- Primary Dark headings on white background
- nuDesk Blue accents for dividers and callout borders
- Table headers: Primary Dark background with white text
- Footer: centered "nuDesk LLC | AI-Workforce Agency for Financial Services"
- Clean, data-forward presentation — tables over prose when data is available

### SOC 2 & Compliance Documents
- Follow the same visual standards as internal memos
- Include document control metadata: version, author, date, classification
- Use "Confidential" watermark for documents containing control details
