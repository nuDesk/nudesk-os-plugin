# PRIME Nexus: Brand Guide

**Last Updated:** December 17, 2025

## 01. Our Brand Essence

Our brand is built for our partners. It is an extension of our platform: intelligent, reliable, and engineered for growth. Every touchpoint should communicate sophistication and a deep understanding of the financial technology landscape.

### Vision
To be the pioneering B2B Partner Platform in Financial Services, enabling PRIME businesses to access the best financial resources and tools at the moment they need them, on the right terms, through our trusted ecosystem of Trusted Advisors.

### Mission
To unite the Trusted Advisors of the Mainstream Business Economy — empowering them to build generational wealth for their PRIME business clients while unlocking value for themselves.

### Market Category
B2B Partner Platform

### Tagline
Growth Together. Win Together.

## 02. Logotype & Usage

### Primary Logotype
The logotype combines a strong, abstract mark with a clean, confident wordmark. The word "PRIME" is de-emphasized with a lighter weight, placing visual focus on **NEXUS**.

### Logomark — The Convergence N
A geometric abstraction formed by two solid, interlocking structures that reveal the letter "N" through negative space. Features a precise linear gradient transitioning from deep forest to vibrant Nexus Green, symbolizing acceleration of business growth.

### Endorsement Lockup (Powered by nuDesk)
- nuDesk logo appears secondary to PRIME Nexus logotype
- Scale: nuDesk logo height should be optically 30% of the PRIME Nexus logotype height
- Vertical Stack: nuDesk sits below PRIME Nexus, separated by a distinct vertical gap
- Horizontal: Used only when vertical space is restricted (e.g., website footers)
- "Powered by" text: **Trebuchet MS** (sentence case)

## 04. Brand Voice & Tone

### The Dual Voice: Leadership vs. Intelligence

1. **The Leaders (Human Voice)**
   - Tone: Entrepreneurial, Visionary, Warm
   - Context: Manifestos, Welcome Emails, Video Scripts
   - Role: To inspire action and invite connection

2. **The Nexus (System Intelligence)**
   - Tone: Predictive, Objective, Concise
   - Context: Dashboard Notifications, Match Alerts, Data Insights
   - Role: To deliver value and facts without fluff. The System speaks in data.

## 05. Imagery & Data Visualization

### a. Abstract Backgrounds
- Style: Minimalist 3D rendering, geometric, abstract
- Rendering: Smooth, polished surfaces and clean lines
- Lighting: Subtle, volumetric lighting with soft glows and gradients
- Colors: Dominated by `#142229` with subtle `#505050` and `#A2C7BF`

### b. Data Flow & Connectivity
- Style: Clean 3D data visualization, technical, modern
- Use Nexus Green (`#40B25F`) to highlight key data points or active connections
- Secondary paths: Data Green (`#A2C7BF`) and Deep Blue (`#2A445C`)

### c. Icons & Interface — Dynamic Gradient Line Art
- Lines feature smooth gradient transitioning to Nexus Green (`#40B25F`)
- Strategic use of negative space implies logic and flow
- Minimalistic and abstract, representing refined engineering

### d. Excluded Styles
- No messy or sketchy line work
- No watercolor or paint effects
- No overly busy or cluttered compositions
- **"No People" Rule:** Avoid human photography in brand materials. Imagery visualizes the system, data, and network. Exception: case studies or testimonials featuring a specific Partner.

### e. Data Visualization: "The Intelligence Layer"
- "Dark Mode Fintech" aesthetic — high contrast, neon data lines against Digital Night
- **PRIME Gold (`#D4AF37`)**: The "Opportunity Gap" / "Delta" between current and future state
- **Nexus Green (`#40B25F`)**: Current state or standard performance metrics
- **Nexus Score Widget**: Circular gauge (0-100) for Deal Quality or Match Strength

## 06. Interface & Behavior

### The "Cyborg Pulse" (Loading States)
Use rhythmic breathing "pulse" effect (opacity shifts or glowing radial gradients) for AI processing. Avoid static spinning wheels.

### Connectivity Lines (Action States)
When referrals are sent or matches made, visualize with a "Light Speed" line trace from Node A to Node B in Nexus Green, fading slowly.

### Hover States
Interactive elements subtly "lift" (y-axis shift) or glow on hover.
